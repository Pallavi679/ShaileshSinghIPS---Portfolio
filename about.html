About Me

A stronger society is the foundation stone for a stronger country : IPS Shailesh Singh

Date of birth: - 18th, February 1965
Place of birth: - Neemach, Madhya Pradesh
B.E. (Electrical) from MACT (now, NIT Bhopal)
Father: - Mr. U.B.Singh, well known mathematician of Madhya Pradesh.
Wife: - Dr Sunita Singh.
Daughter: - Shrutika Singh married to Gagan Gupta both are working in ONGC
Son: - Shashank Singh a professional cricketer.

I have worked in ONGC as an electrical engineer for one year thereafter cracked civil services in first attempt, 1987. I then started my career as a trainee in Virudhunagar district of Tamil Nadu as Assistant Superintendent of Police. Afterwards, I got posted as an SDOP in Tiruppur, the knitwear capital of India. Later I came to Madhya Pradesh and served in Durg as Additional Superintendent of Police. Later I served as the Superintendent of Police in Datia, Seoni and Chhindwara districts. I have also worked as the Commanding Officer of the State Armed Forces in Bilaspur and did the humongous job of shifting the whole battalion from Korba to Sakari in Bilaspur and worked very hard to settle down the complete battalion along with their families. I have served as DIG SAF, Eastern Range, MP, and managed 4 SAF battalions under my leadership. As Inspector General of Police, Narcotics, I have worked hard in combating drug trafficking.

I was the Zonal Joint Director General of Foreign Trade in Mumbai and worked steadfastly for enhancement of exports and for reduction of deficit in foreign trade and also very well in assisting exporters for more foreign export. Later, as IGP home guard in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, I managed distribution and commandment of manpower deployed under whole of Madhya Pradesh. At the same time, I took adequate steps for the welfare of home guard personnel. As Additional Director General, Cyber Crime, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, I worked hard for the establishment of Cyber Headquarters in Madhya Pradesh for prevention and detection of cyber crime, checking of the cyber frauds, bank frauds etc. I was ADG Fire where I worked diligently to maintain all fire stations of Madhya Pradesh. Presently, I am posted as Special DG in Police Reforms at Police Headquarters, Bhopal.
